Who We Are

About SERENE Residences

SERENE Residences is a Chennai-based construction company founded by S. Sanjay Kumar in 2026. Backed by the founder's 7 years of real-site experience across 25+ projects, we bring a rare combination of technical expertise and business acumen to every build.

Our Vision

To become Chennai's most trusted construction partner — known for delivering homes and commercial spaces that blend quality, aesthetics, and lasting value.

Our Mission

To execute every project on time, within budget, and above expectations — with complete transparency, in-house capabilities, and zero compromise on materials.

Our Values

Honesty in every quote. Quality in every brick. Self-reliance in execution. Pride in every handover. Relationships over transactions.
